Common Questions

Before You Reach Out —
Quick Answers

These are the questions we receive most often. If your question isn't here, the form above is the fastest path to an answer.

What's included in the 90-day pilot?›
The pilot includes: full AI fraud detection deployment on a sub-book of your claims (50K–500K lives), zero platform fee (we earn 25% of validated savings only), a live iRonic Health dashboard for your operations and CMO teams, FRA-compliant audit trails on every claim decision, CMO governance layer activated from day one, and a documented ROI report at the 90-day mark showing exact fraud recovered, loss ratio change, and approval time improvement.
How quickly can we go live?›
4 weeks from a signed pilot agreement and data access grant to the first live claim being scored. The process: Week 1 — API mapping and sandbox setup. Week 2 — historical data validation and model calibration. Week 3 — production API connection. Week 4 — dashboard live, CMO layer active, pilot clock starts. Pre-built connectors for GlobeMed, Bupa Egypt, and AXA One Health shorten this further for those TPAs and insurers.
Do you work with smaller insurers (under 100K lives)?›
Yes. We structure pilots by sub-book, not full portfolio. A 50K-life pilot on a specific product line (e.g., group medical claims) is a valid and common starting point. The pilot is designed to prove ROI on a manageable scope, then expand. Many insurers start with their pharmacy claims sub-book — the highest-fraud channel — before expanding to full claims orchestration.
Is iRonic Health a licensed insurer or TPA?›
No — and this is important. iRonic Health is legally classified as a Decision-Support Software Provider (SaaS). Ironic Group does not issue insurance policies, receive premiums, act as a TPA, or process claims on behalf of members. This means: no FRA insurance licence is required for our clients to deploy us, no SLA risk for us being treated as an insurer, and a much faster procurement path than a regulated financial product. We are software that insurers and TPAs use to make their decisions smarter.
What data do we need to share to start the pilot?›
For the sandbox phase (Week 1–2): 3 months of historical claims data (anonymized is acceptable for model calibration), your provider network roster and pricing benchmarks, and member enrollment records for the sub-book. For the production phase (Week 3+): API access to your live claims workflow (REST or batch feed), provider ID mapping, and member eligibility data. A Data Processing Agreement (PDPL-compliant) is signed before any data exchange.
Do you have references or pilot results to share?›
Pilot metrics are available to qualified prospects after signing an NDA — which we execute within 24 hours. We can share anonymized case evidence (specific fraud archetypes detected, EGP recovered, approval time reduction) on a confidential basis with CFOs, CMOs, and CIOs during scoping calls. We do not publicly share client names or specific results without explicit consent.
Can you integrate with our existing TPA or claims system?›
Yes. iRonic Health is API-first — we plug into your existing workflow, not replace it. We support REST APIs, webhooks, and batch feed integrations. We have pre-built connectors for GlobeMed Egypt, Bupa Egypt, and AXA One Health. For other systems, a custom integration is completed during Week 2–3 of the onboarding process. No rip-and-replace, no disruption to your operations team.
